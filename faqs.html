Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

Browse our collection, select your items, add them to cart, and proceed to checkout. Follow the simple steps to complete your purchase.

What are the delivery times?

Standard delivery takes 2-3 business days within Kampala, and 3-5 business days nationwide. Express delivery options are available.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 7 days of delivery. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and with original tags attached. Visit our Returns page for more details.

How do I find my size?

Check our Size Guide for detailed measurements. If you're between sizes, we recommend going for the larger size.

Can I modify my order?

Orders can be modified within 1 hour of placement. Contact our customer service team immediately for assistance.
