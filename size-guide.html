Size Guide

How to Measure

Chest

Measure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping the tape horizontal.

Shoulders

Measure from shoulder point to shoulder point across your back.

Length

Measure from the highest point of the shoulder to desired length.

Size Tips

Measuring Tips

  • Use a flexible measuring tape
  • Keep the tape straight but not tight
  • Measure over light clothing

Perfect Fit

  • If between sizes, go for the larger size
  • Consider your preferred fit (slim/loose)
  • Check specific product descriptions
